# 1.1 The Bridge of Death

- Porre tre domande all'utente:
    - `"What is your name?"`
    - `"What is your quest?"`
    - `"What is your favorite color?"`
- Se le risposte sono `"Lancelot"`, `"Holy Grail"` e `"Blue"`, stampare:
    - `"Right. Off you go."`
- Altrimenti, stampare:
    - `"Down into the Gorge of Ethernal Peril!"`
